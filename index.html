About Me

Hello, I'm Abhigyan, a Machine Learning and Data Science enthusiast currently pursuing my PhD in the Information Systems area at IIT Roorkee.

I am working under the supervision of Prof. Gaurav Dixit, who is a joint faculty member of Mehta Family School of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (MFSDSAI) and Department of Management Studies at IIT Roorkee.

I embarked on my corporate journey at Hindustan Aeronautics Limited as an Apprentice, later transitioning to the role of SYSTEM ENGINEER with a focus on software development in the BFSI domain at TATA CONSULTANCY SERVICES.

After approximately 3 years in the corporate sector, I cultivated a keen interest in research on emerging technologies like Data Science and Artificial Intelligence.

Consequently, I wrote the GATE exam and decided to pursue a PhD with a fusion of Management and Data Science in the Information Systems domain at DoMS, IIT Roorkee.
